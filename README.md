# Neurophysics Projects

## Overview
This repository contains independent neurophysics and biophysics projects focused on understanding neural signal transmission through physical principles.

The work combines theoretical modeling, mathematical formulation, and conceptual exploration of ion-based neural processes.

## Research Focus
The main research themes include:

- Ion dynamics in neural systems  
- Synaptic signal transmission  
- Diffusion-based modeling  
- Physical constraints of neural communication  
- The relationship between physical processes and emergent phenomena such as cognition and consciousness  

## Projects

### 1. Ion Channel Hypothesis
A conceptual and theoretical exploration of how ion channel activity and signal propagation may contribute to higher-level neural phenomena, including questions related to consciousness.

### 2. Extracellular Viscosity Model
A theoretical and computational model investigating how extracellular fluid viscosity affects synaptic transmission speed.

Proposed relation:
v = D / Δx

## Methods
- Theoretical modeling  
- Diffusion and transport-based reasoning  
- Mathematical abstraction  
- Graphical visualization (Desmos)
